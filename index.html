Welcome!

The Cyber Discovery Community is a passionate community of people who are interested in cyber security and technology. This GitHub pages site aims to showcase some of the projects being undertaken by the members of our community. Please feel free to check-out some of our awesome, fully open-source projects!
What is the HMG Cyber Schools programme? What is Cyber Discovery? Can I take part? Find full details regarding the programme here.

Survival Guide

A website where we host tips and tricks for those who are attempting the Cyber Discovery programme. All these articles are written by past participants, most of whom reached the elite stage themselves.

Discord Server

Join the largest Cyber Discovery Discord server with nearly 700 like-minded individuals and some SANS Institute staff members who are running the programme!
